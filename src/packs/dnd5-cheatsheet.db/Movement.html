Movement

  • Move
    Maximum speed, you can fragment your movement
  • Drop prone
    No cost
  • Standing up
    Costs half of your speed
  • Climbing, swimming
    Moving 1ft costs double
  • Difficult terrain
    Moving 1ft costs double
  • Long jump
    Number of feet up to your Strength score if you move (at least at least 10 feet), else half this distance.
  • High jump
    3 + your Strength modifier feet if you move (at least at least 10 feet), else half this distance.
